# Roadmap & Feature Ideas - BookYourGround

This document tracks future features, optimizations, and innovative ideas to evolve BookYourGround into the ultimate sports management platform.

## 🏏 Cricket Scoring & Analytics
- [ ] **Wagon Wheel Visualization**: Interactive 360-degree chart showing where a batter has scored their runs.
- [ ] **Pitch Maps**: Map where every ball from a bowler landed (Short, Good, Full).
- [ ] **AI Match Summary**: Use LLMs to generate a natural language newspaper-style summary of a match based on its commentary logs.
- [ ] **Duckworth-Lewis (DLS) Calculator**: Integrated rain-rule target calculator for professional match management.
- [ ] **Live Streaming Integration**: Overlay live scores on top of a RTMP video stream for YouTube/Facebook broadcasting.

## 🏟️ Venue Management (Owners)
- [ ] **Dynamic Pricing Engine**: Automatically adjust prices based on peak hours, holidays, or last-minute availability (similar to airlines/hotels).
- [ ] **Membership Plans**: Allow grounds to offer monthly/yearly memberships with discounted rates or priority booking.
- [ ] **Inventory & Shop**: Integrated POS system for owners to sell refreshments or rent equipment (bats, balls, jerseys).
- [ ] **Owner Mobile App**: A dedicated, ultra-fast native app for owners to manage bookings on the go.

## 👥 Player & Community Experience
- [ ] **Player Progression System**: RPG-style leveling based on match performance (XP, Achievements, Badges).
- [x] **Team Chat & Coordination**: In-app messaging for team members to discuss strategy and match logistics.
- [ ] **Find a Sub**: A marketplace for teams looking for a last-minute 11th player.
- [ ] **Video Highlights Hub**: High-performance video uploader for users to share their best sixes or wickets.

## 💳 Payments & Finance
- [ ] **Split Payments**: Allow teams to split the booking cost directly in the app before confirming the slot.
- [ ] **Subscription Billing**: Recurring payments for regular users (e.g., a team that plays every Sunday morning).
- [ ] **Loyalty Points**: Earn "GroundCoins" for every booking, redeemable for discounts on future games.

## 🛠️ Platform Optimizations
- [ ] **Offline Scoring Mode**: Allow scorers to record matches without internet, with automatic syncing once back online.
- [ ] **Multi-Sport Engine**: Extend the specialized scoring logic to Football, Badminton, and Padel.
- [ ] **PWA Support**: Full "Install to Home Screen" support with service workers for a native feel on web.
