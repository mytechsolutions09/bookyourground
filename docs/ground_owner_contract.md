## 1. PURPOSE

The purpose of this Agreement is to define the terms under which the Ground Owner agrees to list and make available its sports facility/facilities ("Venue") on the BOOK YOUR GROUND platform including website, mobile application, and associated digital services.

---

## 2. PLATFORM SERVICES

### 2.1 Venue Listing
The Venue shall be displayed on the Platform with relevant information including:
- Venue name & location
- Photographs
- Amenities
- Pricing
- Operating hours
- Slot availability

### 2.2 Booking Management
The Platform shall provide:
- Online booking functionality
- Slot management
- Payment collection
- Booking confirmation system

### 2.3 Dashboard Access
The Partner shall receive access to a partner dashboard for:
- Inventory management
- Slot blocking
- Booking tracking
- Revenue monitoring

### 2.4 Promotions & Marketing
The Platform may promote the Venue through:
- Social media
- Paid advertisements
- Email campaigns
- In-app promotions
- Influencer collaborations

---

## 3. GROUND OWNER OBLIGATIONS

The Ground Owner agrees to:

### 3.1 Accurate Information
Provide complete and accurate information regarding:
- Pricing
- Facilities & dimensions
- Images
- Amenities
- Operating hours

### 3.2 Availability Management
Maintain accurate slot availability at all times and immediately block slots booked through offline channels.

### 3.3 Honor Confirmed Bookings
Honor all bookings confirmed through the Platform without discrimination or additional unauthorized charges.

### 3.4 Venue Maintenance
Maintain the Venue in a safe, clean, operational, and playable condition.

### 3.5 User Experience
Ensure professional conduct toward all users booked through the Platform.

---

## 4. COMMERCIAL TERMS

### 4.1 Commission
The Platform shall charge a commission of:
- ___ % per successful booking, OR
- ₹ ________ flat fee per successful booking

Exclusive of applicable taxes unless otherwise agreed between the Parties.

### 4.2 Payments & Payouts
Payouts shall be processed on a **Weekly / Bi-Weekly / Monthly** basis after deduction of:
- Commission
- Taxes
- Refunds
- Applicable charges

Payments shall be made to the Partner's registered bank account.

---

## 5. CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY

### 5.1 User Cancellation
User cancellations shall follow the Platform's standard cancellation policy.

### 5.2 Venue Cancellation
The Ground Owner shall not cancel confirmed bookings except in cases of:
- Force majeure
- Unsafe weather conditions
- Technical failure
- Government restrictions
- Emergency maintenance

Repeated cancellations may result in:
- Penalties
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent delisting

---

## 6. LIABILITY & INDEMNITY

### 6.1 Venue Responsibility
The Ground Owner shall remain solely responsible for:
- Venue operations
- Player safety & on-ground incidents
- Property management

### 6.2 Indemnification
The Partner agrees to indemnify and hold harmless BOOK YOUR GROUND against:
- Legal claims
- Injuries & damages
- Disputes & losses arising at the Venue

### 6.3 Platform Role
The Platform acts solely as a technology intermediary facilitating bookings.

---

## 7. TERM & TERMINATION

### 7.1 Agreement Term
This Agreement shall remain valid for **1 Year** from the Effective Date and auto-renew thereafter unless terminated.

### 7.2 Termination
Either Party may terminate this Agreement with **30 days written notice**.

The Platform may immediately suspend or terminate the listing in case of:
- Fraud
- Repeated customer complaints
- Denial of confirmed bookings
- Breach of Agreement
